Teaching

At the IUT de Sceaux, I provide the following teaching:

  • Mathematics class in the department TC1, first year.

At the Faculté des Sciences d'Orsay, I provided the following teachings:

  • Exercises sessions in Mathématiques de la modélisation, for students of the Licence Bio.
  • Tutoring sessions in Probability, in the Master 2 MathSV.

At École Polytechnique, I gave the following teachings:

  • Courses and Exercises sessions in Applied mathematics, for foreign students.
  • Tutoring sessions in "Stochastic processes", in the Master 2 MathSV.
  • Introduction to Scilab and to probability theory.
  • Tutoring sessions in Probability theory, in the Master 1 Quantitative Economics and Finance.
  • Creation of quizzes to the course "Aléatoire".

Other activities

  • I organize, together with Nicolas Burq, Gaëtan Chenevier and Yves Cornulier the Colloquium of the Département de Mathématiques d'Orsay.
  • I organized, together with Vincent Bansaye, Pierre Gabriel and Thomas Lepoutre, the Summer School Modélisation Mathématique en Dynamique des Populations et Évolution.
  • A second edition of this school, entitled "PDE and probability for neurosciences and individual movements", will happen on the 4th to 8th of July, 2016, at CIRM.
